Chicken Stir Fry

Chicken Stir Fry

Ingredient Checklist

Ingredients 2 tbsp light brown sugar 1/2 tsp paprika 1/2 tsp garlic powder 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper Salt and pepper 1/4 cup panko breadcrumbs 1/2 cup parsley leaves 2 tbsp unsalted, melted butter 1 1/2 lbs skin on salmon filet 1 tbsp mustard

Cooking Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 425 F
  2. Line baking sheet with tin foil
  3. Mix dry ingredients in a bowl
  4. Mix 1/4 tsp salt, 1/4 tsp pepper, panko, butter, and parsley in another bowl
  5. Place salmon on foil, skin side down
  6. Spread mustard over salmon
  7. Place dry ingredient mixture on salmon
  8. Place panko mixture on top of salmon
  9. Bake 16-17 min, until breadcrumbs are golden brown
