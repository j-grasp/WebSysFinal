Chicken Stir Fry

Chicken Stir Fry

Ingredient Checklist

Ingredients 1lb boneless skinless chicken thigh Pinch of salt and pepper 2 tbsp olive oil 2 cups broccoli florets 1 green bell pepper 1/2 cup sliced carrots 2 tsp ground ginger 2 cloves of garlic
Stir Fry Sauce 1 tbsp corn starch 2tbsp cold water 1/4 cup chicken broth 3 tbsp soy sauce 1 tbsp toated sesame oil 1/2 tsp red pepper flakes

Cooking Steps

  1. Whisk all sauce indgredients together in a bowl
  2. Cut all vegetables into small pieces
  3. Add half of oil into pan and set heat to high
  4. Cut chicken into small cubes and brown in pan
  5. Remove chicken, reduce heat to medium, and add vegetables
  6. After 2 minutes, add chicken, add sauce
  7. Bring sauce to boil, recuce heat, leave one minute
  8. Ready to serve
